Visak Can Help You

Visak is dedicated to supporting individuals facing mental health challenges through guided therapy sessions.

While therapy options are available in India, the costs can be a barrier for many. Session fees typically range from ₹1,500 to ₹8,000 per session, depending on the therapist's expertise and location. On average, individuals with mental health concerns may need 12 to 16 sessions to see significant improvement, yet studies show that only about 50% of patients report recovery based on self-assessed symptom measures. At Visak, we strive to make mental health support accessible and effective for everyone.
